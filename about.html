About

This application accepts as input the Ascendant sign and the House of each of the 9 Planets in one's chart, it then determines the relationship between each planet and displays them on a table.It then scores the table in ways described below.

How It Works

  • Calculates house lords based on the Ascendant sign.
  • Determines natural and temporary relationships between planets.
  • Calculates a compound relationship and a total friendliness score.
  • SOON:.
  • Calculates a benefic friendliness score, which depends on the ascendant.
  • Calculates a malefic friendliness score and a balanced score.
  • Displays the distributions of each score and compares the chart.

Temporary Relationships

Temporary relationships are determined by the placement of planets relative to one another within the twelve houses:

  • If two planets are 2, 3, 4, 10, 11, or 12 houses apart, they are considered temporarily friendly.(One being in the 1st the other in the 2nd, is considered 2 houses apart)
  • If two planets are placed in certain complementary positions, they are also temporarily friendly:
    • One in the 4th house, the other in the 10th
    • One in the 2nd house, the other in the 12th
    • One in the 3rd house, the other in the 11th
  • Otherwise, planets are considered temporarily antagonistic.

Compound Relationships

The compound relationship between two planets combines their natural and temporary relationships:

  • If both natural and temporary relationships are friendly, the planets are considered Extreme Friends.
  • If one relationship is friendly and the other neutral, the result is Friend.
  • If both relationships are antagonistic, the planets are considered Extreme Enemies.
  • If one relationship is antagonistic and the other neutral, the result is Enemy.
  • If one relationship is friendly and the other antagonistic, they are Neutral.

Friendliness Score Calculation

The friendliness score is calculated based on each compound relationship:

  • Extreme Friend: +2
  • Friend: +1
  • Neutral: 0
  • Enemy: -1
  • Extreme Enemy: -2

The minimum score is -65, while the maximum is 51. So we normalize it to a 0 to 1 range.
