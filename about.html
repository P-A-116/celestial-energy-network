About

This application allows users to generate an astrological graph based on Vedic astrology principles. It visualizes the relationships between planets and houses, aspects, and calculates a total friendliness score among the planets. It is a work in progress and many more features will be added in due time.

How It Works

  • Calculates house lords based on the Ascendant sign.
  • Determines natural and temporary relationships between planets.
  • Calculates a compound relationship and a total friendliness score.

Understanding the Graph

The graph displays nodes representing planets and houses. Edge colors indicate relationship types (Graha Sambandhas):

  • Red: Lordship relationships
  • Blue: Aspect relationships
  • Green: Same house relationships
  • Gray: Occupies relationships

Using the Application

To use the application:

  1. Select your Ascendant sign.
  2. Enter the house positions for each planet.
  3. Click "Generate Graph" to view results.
